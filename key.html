#PizzaIsLIfe






So, you've been accepted to graduate school at Michigan State University. Congratulations, and welcome to some of the darkest times of your life!

Grad school pretty much sucks, and Lansing pretty much sucks. One way that you can numb the pain of graduate school is through eating pizza. All the graduate students in CHI have sampled the pizza in Lansing, leading to increases in blood pressure, and the expected "post-baccalaureate paunch."

This is a map of the top pizza choices of the CHI graduate students, arguably the saddest, most pizza-eating graduate students of them all, so you know they're experts. We wish you the best of luck in numbing your pain with mediocre pizza.
