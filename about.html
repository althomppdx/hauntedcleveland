About Haunted Cleveland

The Cleveland Sport Curse isn't the only frightening thing about this city. Explore the map to see haunted locations around the Northern Ohio city.



This map takes you through the top haunted locations in Cleveland, Ohio.

From the 19 spirits at the Historic Variety Theatre, including apparition Clara, a previous theatre performer now seen in a white dress at the back of the auditorium, to the ghost of Mrs. Squire at Squire Castle there are plenty of stories to explore throughout the 16 locations across the haunted city.
